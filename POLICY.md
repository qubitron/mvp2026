# Contoso Stays — Hotel Booking Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

---

## 1. Reservation & Booking

- All reservations must be made through the Contoso Stays website, mobile app, or by calling our reservations team at 1-800-555-STAY.
- A valid credit or debit card is required at the time of booking. Prepaid cards are not accepted.
- Guests must be at least 18 years of age to make a reservation.
- Room availability and rates are subject to change until a booking is confirmed.
- A confirmation email will be sent within 15 minutes of a successful booking.

## 2. Check-In & Check-Out

- **Standard check-in:** 3:00 PM local time.
- **Standard check-out:** 11:00 AM local time.
- Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and are subject to a fee of $25 per hour.
- Valid government-issued photo ID is required at check-in.
- The credit card used for booking must be presented at check-in. If unavailable, an alternative card with matching guest name is required.

## 3. Cancellation Policy

| Booking Type       | Cancellation Window       | Penalty                        |
|--------------------|---------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Standard Rate      | 48 hours before check-in  | No charge                      |
| Standard Rate      | Less than 48 hours        | First night's room rate        |
| Non-Refundable     | Any time                  | Full stay charged              |
| Group (10+ rooms)  | 14 days before check-in   | No charge                      |
| Group (10+ rooms)  | Less than 14 days         | 50% of total reservation value |

- No-shows will be charged the full first night's room rate and the remaining nights will be released.

## 4. Pricing & Payment

- All rates are quoted in the local currency of the property unless otherwise specified.
- Taxes and resort fees are not included in the displayed rate and will be added at checkout.
- Contoso Stays accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.
- A hold of $50 per night (up to $200) will be placed on the guest's card at check-in for incidentals.
- The incidental hold is released within 5–7 business days after check-out.

## 5. Loyalty Program — Contoso Rewards

- Guests earn **10 points per $1 spent** on room charges.
- Points can be redeemed starting at 5,000 points for a free standard night.
- Elite tiers: **Silver** (10 nights/year), **Gold** (25 nights/year), **Platinum** (50 nights/year).
- Gold and Platinum members receive complimentary late check-out (until 2:00 PM) and room upgrades when available.
- Points expire after 24 months of account inactivity.

## 6. Pet Policy

- Pets are welcome at select Contoso Stays properties marked as "Pet-Friendly."
- A maximum of 2 pets per room is allowed, each weighing no more than 50 lbs.
- A non-refundable pet fee of $75 per pet per stay applies.
- Service animals are exempt from pet fees and weight restrictions per applicable law.

## 7. Smoking Policy

- All Contoso Stays properties are 100% smoke-free indoors, including e-cigarettes and vaporizers.
- Designated outdoor smoking areas are available at select properties.
- A cleaning fee of $250 will be charged if evidence of smoking is found in a guest room.

## 8. Damages & Liability

- Guests are responsible for any damage to the room or hotel property during their stay.
- Contoso Stays is not liable for loss or theft of personal belongings. In-room safes are provided for valuables.
- Noise complaints after 10:00 PM may result in a warning; repeated violations may result in eviction without refund.

## 9. Accessibility

- Contoso Stays is committed to providing accessible accommodations in compliance with ADA and local regulations.
- Accessible rooms can be requested during booking or by contacting the property directly.
- Requests are fulfilled on a best-effort basis; guests with specific needs are encouraged to book in advance.

## 10. Modifications & Disputes

- Reservation modifications (dates, room type) are subject to availability and may result in a rate change.
- Billing disputes must be raised within 30 days of check-out by contacting support@contosostays.com.
- Contoso Stays reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Changes take effect for bookings made after the updated effective date.

---

*For questions about this policy, contact Contoso Stays Guest Services at support@contosostays.com or 1-800-555-STAY.*
